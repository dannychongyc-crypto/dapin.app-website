Host features

Everything you need to run your community.

Dap IN gives you the tools to plan sessions, group people into circles, collect payments, and keep everyone coming back, without the spreadsheet-and-group-chat juggling.

See the full host guide →

The problem

Running a community is harder than it should be.

Between scattered group chats, chasing payments, last-minute no-shows, and splitting people into groups by hand, the admin quietly eats the time you wanted to spend with your people. Dap IN takes that off your plate.

Features

Built for the way hosts actually work.

Circle split

Big turnout? Circle split automatically divides your attendees into balanced circles of 4 to 8, so every group stays small enough to actually connect. You set the rules.

Member management

Your host dashboard shows every member, their attendance history, and pending join requests in one place. Approve, message, or group people in a couple of taps.

Payments & payouts

Set a price per spot and Dap IN handles collection and payouts for you. Session revenue lands in your host wallet, ready to withdraw to your bank.

Group chat & broadcasts

Every session gets its own group chat, and you can broadcast updates so attendees always know where to be and what to bring.

Reminders & waitlists

Automatic reminders cut down no-shows, and waitlist spots confirm themselves the moment space opens up, so your sessions stay full.

Your own community

Run your community your way. Keep it invite-only for your crew, or open your sessions to all of Dap IN and grow.

Why hosts choose Dap IN

Less admin, more connection.

Less admin

Grouping, chats, reminders, and payments run themselves, so you can focus on the people, not the logistics.

More connection

Small circles by design mean everyone actually talks to everyone, and your community keeps coming back.

Real income

Charge what your sessions are worth. Many hosts cover their costs; others run hosting as a genuine side income.

Get started

Ready to start your community?

Create an account, head to Settings, and tap "Build your own community." You could be hosting your first session this week.
