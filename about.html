About us

Your city is more social than it looks.

Kuala Lumpur is a city full of people and full of things to do, but most of it stays hidden. The best gatherings, whether a hike, a supper club, or a board-game night, happen quietly among people who already know each other. We wanted to make finding your circle more accessible, so we built a platform where anyone can join events around KL and find their people.

"The activity is the door. The connection is the product."

Our mission is simple: make it effortless for people to find their people. Every session on Dap IN, whether a hike, a chess night, or a pickleball run, is really just an excuse to put the right people in the same room and let the rest happen naturally.

What we believe

Three things we won't compromise on.

Small by design

Not every event is small, but the best connections are made in small circles. So when a group gets big, we split it into smaller ones, small enough that everyone talks to everyone and no one is left at the edge.

Host-led

Real people running real experiences. Every session has a host who sets the tone, keeps it welcoming, and makes sure it actually happens.

IRL first

We exist to get you offline. The app is just the doorway. Success for us is you putting your phone away because you're busy having a good time.
