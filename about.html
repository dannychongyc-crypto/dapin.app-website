About us

We built Dap IN because making friends as an adult is genuinely hard.

Dap IN started with one founder in Kuala Lumpur noticing the same quiet problem everywhere: a city full of people, and so many of them lonely in it. The friendships that stuck were never made through apps or small talk. They were made through doing things together. So we built a platform around exactly that: shared activities and small, curated circles.

"The sport is the door. The connection is the product."

Our mission is simple: make it effortless for people to find their people. Every session on Dap IN, whether a hike, a chess night, or a pickleball run, is really just an excuse to put the right people in the same room and let the rest happen naturally.

What we believe

Three things we won't compromise on.

Small by design

Circles of 4 to 8, intentionally curated. Small enough that everyone talks to everyone, and no one gets left standing at the edge of the group.

Host-led

Real people running real experiences. Every session has a host who sets the tone, keeps it welcoming, and makes sure it actually happens.

IRL first

We exist to get you offline. The app is just the doorway. Success for us is you putting your phone away because you're busy having a good time.
