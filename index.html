What is Dap IN

The app that gets you off the app.

Dap IN is a platform for discovering and joining curated social circles and community events across KL: sport, food, chess, hiking, and more. Every session is hosted by a real person and built around doing something together, not endless chat. You browse, you join, you show up. Real people. Real plans.

Built for people who'd rather show up than scroll.

The Social Starter

Looking to expand your circle beyond work and home. Whether you're new to the city or just tired of seeing the same faces, Dap IN puts you in rooms with people who share your energy.

The One With Hobbies

You play sport, you have interests, and you make your best friends through doing things together, not through small talk. Dap IN is built exactly for that.

User guide

Everything you're wondering, answered.

Download Dap IN from the App Store or Play Store and create your profile in under two minutes. Tell us what you're into, whether that's sports, food, games, or the outdoors, and we'll start showing you sessions that fit.

Open the Discover tab and browse by activity, neighbourhood, or date. You can also join communities to stay updated with their announcements and events.

Once your spot is confirmed, you're placed into a circle with the other people attending. You'll get the session details, a group chat, and a reminder before it starts, so all that's left is to show up.

A circle is the small group you're matched into for a session, usually four to eight people. It's intentionally small so everyone actually talks to everyone, and if it clicks, your circle can keep meeting up.

Absolutely. You can invite friends to a session by sharing it with them, and they can join you there.

Every host sets their own cancellation policy, so feel free to contact the host directly to arrange a cancellation.
