Host guide

Host something worth showing up for.

Whether you run a community of 5 or 500, Dap IN gives you the tools to host better. To get started, create an account, head to Settings, and tap "Build your own community."

Ways to host

Pick the lane that fits you.

Virtual Host

Dap IN hosts for you. You just show up and play. Perfect if you want the social credit of bringing people together without the admin.

Community Host

How to get started

From idea to first session in five steps.

1

Create your host profile

Tell people who you are and what you love hosting.

2

Set up your community or session

Pick the activity, venue, time, and group size.

3

Invite members or go public

Keep it to your crew, or open your session to all of Dap IN.

4

Dap IN provides grouping and chat functions

You can group your attendees into circles and keep them in the loop.

5

You show up and host

Do the part only you can do: make it a great time.
